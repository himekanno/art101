Challenge

Our intitial challenges were coming up with tasks to pseudocode. For baking cookies, it was strange to break down the task into individual actions. We had to rethink how one would describe a recipe.

Problems

Our main issue with the hangman game was figuring out how to word the steps the steps the computer needed to take, instead of the player.

Results

Task1

  1. Look at the recipe
  2. Collect ingredients
  3. Select required baking tools
  4. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit
  5. Mix dry ingredients in the first bowl
  6. Set aside that bowl
  7. Mix butter and sugar in a second bowl
  8. Add eggs and vanilla extract
  9. Beat until it has a fluffy consistency
  10. Mix in chocolate chips
  11. Mix ingredients in the first bowl with the ingredients in the second bowl until well combined
  12. Roll ball of cookie dough
  13. Place rolled dough ball on the cookie sheet
  14. Repeat steps 12 and 13 until the dough runs out
  15. Put the sheet in the oven
  16. Bake in the oven until cookies turn golden brown
  17. Take out the pan with cookies
  18. Leave cookies on the sheet for 2 minutes
  19. Place cookies on the cooling rack for 5 minutes
  20. Eat cookies

This is an outline for how to make a chocolate chip cookie.
